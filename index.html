About Me

Hello and thank you for visiting my homepage. I am a first year MAE PhD student at Cornell University. I completed my Bachelor's and Master's program in Aerospace Engg. from IIT Bombay (Mumbai, India) in 2019.

I am fascinated by robots and autonomous vehicles. My research interests lie in the domain of multi-agent control, adaptive control, robotics and learning. For my master's thesis, I worked with Prof. Srikant Sukumar, Systems and Control Engg. at IIT Bombay, on developing an adaptive controller for consensus of networked multi agent system under unknown measurement biases.

Another field that intrigues me is Astrophysics and I have completed a minor degree in Physics. I love reading with an inclination towards mystery and fiction novels. In my free time I play badminton, bicycle and write poetry. I have served as a chief editor for the first ever department newsletter “Lift-off” of Aerospace Dept. at IIT Bombay. We have covered diverse issues of importance to the Aerospace industry and published what is hoped would be an interesting read.
