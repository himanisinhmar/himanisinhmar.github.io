About Me

Hello and thank you for visiting my homepage. I am an incoming MAE PhD student at Cornell University. I completed my Bachelor's and Master's program in Aerospace Engg. from IIT Bombay (Mumbai, India) in 2019.

I am fascinated by space exploration and autonomous aerial vehicles. My research interests primarily lie in the domain of cooperative control, nonlinear adaptive control, and autonomous guidance and navigation of aerial vehicles. I am also keen to work on the amalgamation of reinforcement learning and decentralized control of multi-agent system in an uncertain environment. Currently, I am working with Prof. Srikant Sukumar, Systems and Control Engg. at IIT Bombay, on developing an adaptive controller for consensus of networked multi agent non linear system under unknown measurement biases.

Another field that intrigues me is Astrophysics. I am fascinated by this colossal and mysterious universe and hence have completed a minor degree in Physics. I love reading with an inclination towards mystery and fiction novels. In my free time I love to play badminton, cycle and write poetry. I have served as a chief editor for the first ever department newsletter “Lift-off” of Aerospace Dept. at IIT Bombay. We have covered diverse issues of importance to the Aerospace industry and published what is hoped would be an interesting read.
