Welcome

Hello and thank you for visiting my homepage. I am a final year student enrolled in Integrated Bachelor's and Master's program of Aerospace Engineering at IIT Bombay (Mumbai, India).

I am fascinated by space exploration and autonomous aerial vehicles. My research interests lie in the domain of cooperative control, non linear adaptive control, and autonomous guidance & navigation of aerial vehicles. I am also keen to work on amalgamation of reinforcement learning and decentralized control of multi-agent system in uncertain environment. Currently, I am working with Prof. Srikant Sukumar, Systems and Control Engineering at IIT Bombay, on developing an adaptive controller for consensus of networked multi agent non linear system under unknown measurement biases.

Other fields that intrigue me is Astrophysics. I am fascinated by this colossal and mysterious universe and hence have pursued a minor degree in Physics. I am an amateur reader and love mystery thrillers. In my pastime I love to do activities like trekking, camping, playing badminton and writing.

I have served as a chief editor for the first ever department newsletter Lift-off of Aerospace Dept. at IIT Bombay. We have covered diverse issues of importance to the Aerospace industry and published what is hoped would be an interesting read.
